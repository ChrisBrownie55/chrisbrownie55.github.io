COUNSELORS

HELPING STUDENTS ACHIEVE THEIR GOALS

Erinn Urquiaga

Mrs. Urquiaga is our school's High School, College, and Career Counselor. This is her 7th year at Idaho Arts. She is available to discuss and help with high school graduation requirements, scheduling on-line courses, answering questions regarding the state of Idaho's advanced opportunities program, and assisting you and your child with college and career planning.

Karie Yost

Ms. Yost is our school's Middle School Counselor and Instructional Coach. This is her 11th year at Idaho Arts Charter School and 19th year in education. She is very excited to be a part of the IACS family. She received her elementary degree and special education endorsement from Boise State and her Masters in school counseling from NNU.

Rikki Clemens

Ms. Clemens is our school's Kindergarten through Fourth counselor, this is her second year as a school counselor here at IACS. She received her Masters degree in school and mental health counseling from Capella University while working as a Family Preservation Specialist. She is excited to see all the returning students and to meet all of the new kids.
